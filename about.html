About Us

Australia is abundant with unique experiences and awe-inspiring landscapes. There’s a well-known vibrancy in its natural beauty, but don’t forget to seek out its history and culture as well. There's plenty here to inspire your future travel plans so, go on, take a look around and let yourself dream of all the possibilities.

Ignite your wanderlust with our video series and see all Australia has to offer. From extraordinary bush regeneration, cute and quirky wildlife and awe-inspiring landscapes, to getting a sneak peek into the new and unexpected, it’s all part of what makes Australia one of the most unique destinations in the world.

Take a moment to daydream and keep your wanderlust alive. Discover weird and wonderful wildlife, extraordinary bush regeneration and awe-inspiring natural landscapes. And the best part? While we wait for you, the best bits are only getting better. Imagine that.

In response to COVID-19 (Coronavirus), Australia’s border is temporarily closed. Currently, only Australian citizens, residents and immediate family members are permitted to travel to Australia. All travellers arriving in Australia are required to complete 14 days of quarantine in a designated facility (such as a hotel) in their port of arrival. For more information, visit the Department of Health website.

In the meantime, there’s plenty to explore before your dream trip Down Under becomes a reality. So stay inspired and know that we can’t wait to see you.
